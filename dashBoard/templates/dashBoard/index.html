About Us

Fundraising for medical treatment is largely online in India and for patients with late stage disease with very visible symptoms. Some platforms are working on online fundraisers for only children, but again late stage disease. The number of cases accepted is only one out of ten if not one out of twenty. This leaves a large majority of patients with no option than to give up treatment or fend for themselves. There are no funding, financial access options available for this large majority of patients that get rejected by online platforms. We are hoping to build a corpus to help these patients with funding options to help them get treated.
